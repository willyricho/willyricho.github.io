MTD for IT – AI Avatars

For the MTD for IT campaign, I was tasked with developing a workflow to create realistic AI avatars that could walk through Xero’s MTD capabilities in an engaging, approachable way. The goal was to make complex product information feel human and relatable, while keeping production fast and scalable.

After a period of testing and experimentation, I built an end-to-end avatar pipeline that combined AI-generated characters, synthetic voiceovers, and motion design. I started by designing photo-real characters, then generated natural-sounding voiceovers using ElevenLabs and produced the avatar performances through HeyGen. The final stage involved compositing and refining each scene in After Effects, where I layered motion design and animated UI elements to enhance storytelling and visual interest.

AI Process Insight

Creating lifelike avatars required a mix of experimentation, iteration, and technical precision. I began by generating photo-real characters using ChatGPT and Visual Electric, which allowed me to establish the look, personality, and tone for each avatar. Once the characters were finalised, I isolated them in Photoshop and added Xero clothing to ensure brand consistency.

Next, I created natural, human-sounding AI voiceovers in ElevenLabs, then generated the avatar performances in HeyGen, syncing them closely with the audio. I brought everything together in After Effects, where I composited the avatars into each scene, integrated motion graphics elements and UI animations, and refined lighting and sound for realism.

The process ended with rounds of review and iteration to ensure each avatar felt genuine, approachable, and aligned with the storytelling tone of the MTD for IT campaign.
