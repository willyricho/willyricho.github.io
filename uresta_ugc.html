Uresta - UGC Social Video Series

I created a series of short UGC-style videos for Uresta, editing down longer testimonial clips into tight pieces designed for social platforms. The goal was to maintain the genuine feel of UGC while giving each piece enough structure, pacing, and visual polish to perform well on social channels.
